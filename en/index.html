About the Firm

Operating in the field of legal services for more than 27 years.

Law office "Vindex" is a bureau with more than 25 years of experience in the field of commercial law and especially construction law. A carefully prepared contract is a prerequisite for a successful business transaction, while regular legal monitoring ensures timely risk prevention.
