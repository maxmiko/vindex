Cookie Policy

Effective from: January 1, 2025

This cookie policy explains how SIA "Vindex" uses cookies on its website vindex.lv.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit a website. They help the site remember your settings and improve your browsing experience.

2. Cookies We Use

Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function. They provide basic functionality such as saving your cookie consent preferences.

cookie_consent Stores your cookie preference 1 year

Analytics Cookies (only with your consent)

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website. Information is collected anonymously.

_ga, _ga_* Google Analytics — visit statistics 2 years

3. Managing Cookies

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" button at the bottom of the website or by changing your browser settings.

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. However, disabling all cookies may limit website functionality.

4. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this cookie policy. Changes will be published on this page with an updated date.
