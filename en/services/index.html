Services

We offer the following services

Over 25 years of experience in commercial and construction law allows us to deliver precise and well-considered solutions. Regardless of complexity — we work to the highest standards.

01

Contract Law

The firm has extensive experience in preparing, evaluating, adjusting and coordinating both local and cross-border contract projects. We have provided legal opinions on already concluded contracts and the risks associated with their performance. When working with contract projects, various development scenarios are modelled and recommendations are made regarding risks and further steps. We represent clients in contract negotiations, coordinate positions with counterparties, and help clients understand all risks when entering into contracts.

02

Construction Issues

The firm has provided legal support for the largest construction projects in independent Latvia — the National Library, the second railway track in Tīnūži–Krustpils, Krievu Island, the Southern Bridge and others. We have practical experience ensuring balanced cooperation, timely and quality construction, and avoiding unnecessary litigation.

Get in touch
03

Commercial Law

We provide a full spectrum of legal services for business operations — company formation and registration, shareholders' meetings, dividend distribution and shareholder dispute resolution. We have implemented complex reorganisations and were the first in Latvia to transfer a company's legal address from one EU member state to another.

Get in touch
04

M&A Transactions

The firm has considerable experience in company acquisition and merger transactions, representing both buyers and sellers. We conduct legal due diligence, identifying acquisition risks and helping clients secure more favourable terms. We handle the full preparation, evaluation and adjustment of all transaction documentation.

Get in touch
05

Public Procurement

The firm has been active in public procurement for many years — representing tenderers, preparing tenders, clarifying contract drafts, challenging decisions, and advising on procurement organisation and requirements. We help prepare complaints to the Procurement Monitoring Bureau and have achieved positive outcomes including changes in case law through successful appeals.

Get in touch
06

Competition Law

We work on matters regulated by the Competition Law — developing internal compliance regulations, representing client interests before the Competition Council and in court, and preparing merger notifications. We advise on all competition-related issues of interest to clients.

Get in touch
07

Real Estate

We provide legal support on all real estate matters — purchase, sale, leasing, rental, establishment, amendment or cancellation of easements, division or consolidation of properties, joint ownership issues, and property management, tenancy and lease matters.

Get in touch
08

Tax Law

We advise on various tax law matters and recommend optimal tax planning solutions when preparing transactions. We assist with SRS registrations and document submissions, and represent clients in disputes with tax authorities. The firm has experience representing clients before the Court of Justice of the EU in Luxembourg, resulting in a favourable ruling.

Get in touch
09

Employment Law

We draft employment contracts, non-compete agreements and internal employer regulations. We assist in resolving labour disputes and handling termination and reinstatement of employment. We have successfully represented clients in court and contributed to the development of case law in this field.

Get in touch
10

Immigration Law

We advise on immigration matters and assist with visas, temporary and permanent residence permits. We have helped obtain refugee status and successfully challenged extradition orders. The firm has experience before the European Court of Human Rights, where we secured a favourable judgment and compensation for the client.

Get in touch
11

Family & Succession Law

We provide legal assistance on divorce, division of marital property, recovery of contributions made to the other spouse's separate property, custody and access rights. We represent clients before the Orphan's Court and in court, and work on international cases involving jurisdiction issues and child abduction matters.

We advise on succession law matters and have extensive experience in cross-border inheritance cases where heirs and estate assets are located in multiple countries, requiring proceedings in courts of different jurisdictions.

Get in touch
12

Administrative Law

We assist clients on administrative law and administrative procedure matters. We prepare applications for challenging and appealing administrative acts and the actual conduct of institutions. We represent client interests in administrative proceedings before institutions and courts.

Get in touch
13

Litigation

We assist clients in all cases where disputes have reached the court. Although litigation is not our firm's primary specialisation, we have accumulated extensive experience successfully defending clients' interests in complex cases. In matters where we cannot provide the best possible assistance, we inform clients and help attract the relevant specialists.
