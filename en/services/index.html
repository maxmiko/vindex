Contract law

The office has long-term experience in preparation, evaluation, correction and coordination of both local and cross-border draft contracts. We represent clients in contract negotiations, coordinate positions with contractual partners, as well as help the client to be aware of all risks when concluding contracts.

Public procurements

The office has been active in the field of public procurements for many years – representing both applicants participating in announced procurements, assisting in drawing up tenders, adjusting draft contracts, contesting the decisions, as well as consulting entrepreneurs and subjects of public law in connection with the organisation of procurements, setting requirements for candidates or applicants, amending the contracts, as well as other issues. We help preparing complaints to the Procurement Monitoring Office, as well as represent clients in examination of complaints in the institution and in court. By appealing decisions unfavourable to the clients, we have achieved positive decisions, including case law changes in respect of interpretation and application of regulatory enactments in the field of procurements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Our Services

What forms of cooperation does Vindex offer?

The firm offers various forms of cooperation — hourly-rate legal assistance, project-based engagements, and monthly retainer arrangements at a fixed fee. The most suitable model is selected for each client depending on their needs and the scale of their business activities.

Is the first consultation really free?

Yes, the first consultation is free of charge. It allows us to understand the situation and helps the client determine what legal assistance they need and what the optimal model for ongoing cooperation would be.

How long does it take to resolve a matter?

The timeframe depends on the complexity of the matter and the area of law. Straightforward consultations can be provided promptly, while complex commercial or litigation matters may take considerably longer. A realistic timeframe estimate is provided to the client for each matter.

Does the firm handle international matters?

Yes, the firm handles international matters — including cross-border contracts, international jurisdiction issues, and foreign corporate structures. The firm has experience representing clients before the Court of Justice of the European Union and the European Court of Human Rights.
